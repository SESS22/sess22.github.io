  • Started in 2022 Summer
  • Currently operated as a club under SSF
  • Has club members from other programs as well
  • A community representing the engineering student body in Seneca.
  • Ran by a team of 9 students + 1 faculty advisor
  • Bi-weekly meeting
  • In the stage of transitioning to an independent Engineering Student Society

What does SESS do?

The aim of SESS is to provide aid to the professional and social development of tomorrow's engineers.

Events & Services

  • Frosh week event
  • National Engineering Month (NEM) Event
  • Engineering Competition/Challenge
  • Social Events & Workshops
  • A bridge between the students and external organizations such as OSPE, ESSCO & CFES
